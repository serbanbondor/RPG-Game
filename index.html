Battle Royale

Choose your hero!

Rogue

They use energy as resource and the primary stat is agility which gives them a chance to do double the damage. Rogues deal small amounts of damage but they can attack multiple times in one turn.

Mage

The mage uses mana as resource, intellect is the primary stat and they use both of them to cast spells. They use spells to do a lot of damage

Hunter

They use focus as a resource and their primary stat is agility. The hunter uses bows and arrows to do ranged attacks which can do double damage
